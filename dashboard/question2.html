NAEP

The NAEP (aka “The Nation’s Report Card”) is a test that measures the proficiency of U.S. students in various academic subjects. An obvious limitation of this data is that it’s for students in the U.S, not Canada. This is a congressionally mandated project administered by the National Center for Education Statistics under the U.S Department of Education. The test is administered during school hours to a representative sample of students from public / nonpublic schools. Students are provided with all necessary materials/technologies to complete the assessment at school.
