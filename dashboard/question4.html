Methodology

For this question, we gathered data regarding study time for students from the Institute for Financial Studies and the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development. To determine if the significance between the mean of study time before vs during COVID-19 was statistically significant, we used Welch's Two Sample T-Test. We chose this because it is more reliable when you have unequal variances or sample sizes.

Results

Our results indicate a very large p-value. As a result, we cannot conclude if the change in study time before vs during COVID-19 is statistically significant. The results of this question will be analyzed alongside the results of Question 3 and 5 to determine if academic performance of students changed as a result of COVID-19 and if low-income populations are more vulnerable to changes than their higher income counterparts.
